Speaking — Practice Prompts

Practice short answers (Part 1), long turn (Part 2) and discussion questions (Part 3).

Part 2 (Long Turn)

Describe a memorable journey you took. You should say: where you went, who you went with, what happened, and explain why it was memorable.

✓ Tips
  • Make quick bullet points, then speak for 1–2 minutes.
  • Use linking phrases and vary vocabulary.

Part 1 (Short questions)

  • Do you enjoy reading? Why/Why not?
  • What type of music do you listen to?
  • How often do you use public transport?

Part 3 (Discussion)

These are longer questions related to the Part 2 topic — discuss causes, effects and opinions.

  • How do travel experiences influence people’s careers?
  • Are there benefits to travelling alone compared to with family?
✓ Practice tip

Record a 2-minute answer for the Part 2 cue card and listen for filler words, pronunciation, and coherence.
